## Binary Operators

Binary operators work on two values.

### Arithmetic

- `+`: add
- `-`: subtract
- `*`: multiply
- `/`: divide
- `%`: remainder
- `//`: floor division

### Comparison

- `==`: equals
- `!=`: not equals
- `<`: less than
- `<=`: less than or equal
- `>`: greater than
- `>=`: greater than or equal

### Logic

- `and`: logical AND
- `or`: logical OR

### Assignment

- `=`: assign
- `+=`: add and assign
- `-=`: subtract and assign
- `*=`: multiply and assign
- `/=`: divide and assign
- `%=`: remainder and assign
- `//=`: floor and assign

### Null Handling

- `?:`: use `y` when `x` is null, otherwise use `x`
